Assignment 1

Artist: Leonard Solaas

Leonard Solaas is a self-taught artist and programmer from Argentina who creates interactive net-art pieces through data visualization and generative systems.

His work has been exhibited in many places around the world including at International Media Art Award in Germany, Impakt festival in Holland, and 5ta Bienal del Mercosur in Brasil among others.

Many of his pieces use image recognition to analyze and create abstract works based off of our surroundings. My personal favourite being Dreamlines where any search term is given to result in a continuously moving, computer drawn piece of art based on popular image results for the term.

Leonard's other works range from abstract geometric shapes to patterns generated by wave interference
