Hobbies

Music

From junior high band class to performing at paid gigs around the city, music has comprised a big part of my life ever since I was young. I play a variety of instruments ranging from strings like bass and guitar to drums and bass clarinet.
In my spare time, I enjoy playing around with new sounds in Ableton live and messing around on my bass, music acts as a great way to wind down and relax after a long day.

Skateboarding

Although decidedly an amateur, I've been enjoying learning how to perform tricks on my skateboard over the past couple years. When I've got time and the weather's right I also like to get out on my longboard to enjoy the parks and fresh air around the city!

Video Games

After my classes or just when I'm feeling the urge, I like to relax by playing some single-player video games such as my favorite, Hollow Knight. The freedom games bring to play however you want and explore remarkable stories is a great way to gain some relief from the stress of our everyday lives.
